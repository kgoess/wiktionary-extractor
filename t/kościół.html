[edit] Polish

kościół

[edit] Etymology

From Old Czech kostel, Old High German kástel, from Latin castellum

[edit] Pronunciation

  • IPA: /ˈkɔɕt​͡ɕuw̯/
  • audio
    		(file)

[edit] Noun

kościół m.

  1. Church (a Christian house of worship, usually with reference to Catholicism. An Eastern Rite church is referred to as a cerkiew, a Protestant one as a zbór)
  2. Church (A Christian religious organisation)
